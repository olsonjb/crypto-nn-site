Blockchain Assets

Crystal Crypto invests exclusively in blockchain technologies. Our neural networks are trained to make predictions for Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum and Bitcoin Cash assets.

A Deep Neural Network

Crystal is our name for our recurrent deep neural network, trained on hundreds of thousands of datapoints representing the historical values of cryptocurrencies over time. Recurrent neural networks use short term memory to make predictions based on current trends and are particularly useful when working with time series data (such as blockchain asset or stock values).

Precision Results

Changes in asset prices are notoriously difficult to predict, and blockchain assets are particularly volatile. However, more volatile assets offer greater opportunity to realize high returns. By making frequent trades on a network with our accuracy rates, we can mitigate risk and provide high returns with remarkably high reliability.
